About Me

I’m a Digital Designer and Developer at the Royal Ontario Museum and a graduate of HackerYou’s Web Development Bootcamp.

I love building beautiful, compelling websites and engaging in the complex problem-solving that web development so often requires. Using powerful tools like JavaScript and React is my favourite way to create a strong narrative experience for every project. I thrive under deadlines and pressure, a skill honed while managing the publication of over 80 books at an indie publisher. I’m looking for opportunities at a company where best practices are valued and professional development is encouraged.

When I’m not coding, you can find me binge reading the latest fantasy series, making new dog friends, and trying to be in the presence of piney trees as much as possible.
