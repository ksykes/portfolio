About Me

I’m a front-end web developer based in Toronto, ON and a graduate of HackerYou’s Front End Web Development Bootcamp. I love learning so much I also have a B.A. in English Literature and an M.A. in Public Texts. My voracious curiosity has been invaluable as a professional Googler / developer.

When I’m not coding, you can find me learning how to knit for the third time, reading the latest fantasy novel, snoozing in the park, making new dog friends, and trying to be in the presence of piney trees as much as possible.
